The Different Types of Roblox Games

  1. Roleplay
  2. Adventure
  3. Fighting
  4. Obby
  5. Tycoon
  6. Simulator

Roleplay

Adventure

Pretty much includes anything and everything having to do with adventure! An example of this type of game is Jailbreak by Badimo:

Obby

Tycoon

Ever thought what it's like to be a princess or run a car dealership. Well you can do that and much more in Roblox's tycoon games. An example of this type of game is Super Hero Tycoon by Super Heroes:

Simulator
